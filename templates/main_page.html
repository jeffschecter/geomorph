Keyboard commands!


LEFT, UP, RIGHT — Highlight the left, top, or right face of the cube. Or, move the cursor one unit in the highlit direction.

DOWN — Move the cursor one unit opposite the highlit direction.

0 — Return the cursor to the origin.

d — Delete the highlit tile.


CLICK a tile in the palette to the left to drop it on the map.
